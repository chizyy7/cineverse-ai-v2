# CineVerse AI — User Onboarding System

Upon registration, users complete a personalization process across four stages.

---

## Stage 1 — Entertainment Selection

Users choose which content types they are interested in:

- Movies
- Anime
- TV Shows
- Music
- Podcasts

---

## Stage 2 — Genre Selection

Users select their preferred genres across all chosen entertainment types.

---

## Stage 3 — Favorite Content Selection

Users provide:

- Favorite movies
- Favorite anime
- Favorite artists
- Favorite actors
- Favorite shows

---

## Stage 4 — Mood Preferences

Users indicate preferred emotional experiences:

| Mood | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Emotional | Deep, feeling-driven content |
| Funny | Comedy and lighthearted content |
| Inspirational | Motivating and uplifting content |
| Dark | Intense, heavy, or gritty themes |
| Mind-blowing | Thought-provoking or twist-heavy content |
| Relaxing | Calm and easy-going content |
| Intense | High-stakes or action-packed content |
| Family-friendly | Safe for all ages |
