# CineVerse AI — AI Assistant

Users can interact with a conversational AI assistant to discover content through natural language.

---

## How It Works

Instead of browsing menus or filters, users describe what they want in their own words. The AI interprets the request, matches it against the user's Entertainment DNA, and returns personalized recommendations with explanations.

---

## Example Queries

> *"I want a movie like Interstellar but easier to understand."*

> *"Recommend a gym playlist."*

> *"I need a dark anime with amazing fights."*

---

## Key Differentiator

The AI does not simply display a list of results. It **explains why** each recommendation was generated, helping users understand the connection between their taste and the suggestion.

This creates a more trustworthy and engaging discovery experience.
