# CineVerse AI — Mood-Based Discovery

Users can discover content by selecting a **mood** rather than a specific genre or category.

---

## How It Works

The user expresses their current emotional state or desired experience. The AI maps that mood to relevant content across all entertainment categories simultaneously.

---

## Example Mood Inputs

| Mood Input | What the AI Does |
|------------|-----------------|
| "I am sad." | Surfaces comforting movies, uplifting music, feel-good anime |
| "I want something inspiring." | Recommends motivational podcasts, biographical films, sports anime |
| "I need pure action." | Pulls high-energy movies, intense anime, workout playlists |

---

## Multi-Category Output

A single mood request can generate recommendations across:

- Movies
- Anime
- Music playlists
- Podcasts

This cross-category response is unique to CineVerse AI and allows users to build a complete entertainment session around a single feeling.
