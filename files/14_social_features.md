# CineVerse AI — Social Features

CineVerse AI includes community and social features to enhance the discovery experience through shared taste.

---

## What Users Can Do

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Follow Friends | Stay connected with users who share similar taste |
| Share Watchlists | Share curated content collections with others |
| Share Playlists | Share music or podcast collections |
| Write Reviews | Publish opinions on movies, shows, anime, and more |
| Like Reviews | Upvote reviews from other community members |
| Comment on Recommendations | Discuss AI-generated picks with others |

---

## Why Social Matters

Social signals enrich the collaborative filtering model — when users with similar taste engage with the same content, the recommendation engine becomes more accurate for everyone.

Community activity also helps surface hidden gems that algorithmic signals alone might miss.
