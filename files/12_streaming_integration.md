# CineVerse AI — Streaming & Listening Integration

CineVerse AI **does not host copyrighted content**.

---

## What CineVerse AI Provides

- Content metadata
- Descriptions and synopses
- Reviews and ratings
- AI-generated recommendations and match scores

---

## How Users Access Content

After receiving a recommendation, users are redirected to legitimate streaming and listening services where the content is available.

---

## Supported Platforms (Examples)

| Type | Platform |
|------|----------|
| Video Streaming | Netflix |
| Anime Streaming | Crunchyroll |
| Music | Spotify |
| Music | Apple Music |
| Video | YouTube |
| Others | Additional supported providers |

---

## Why This Model Works

By acting as a **discovery layer** rather than a content host, CineVerse AI:

- Avoids copyright and licensing complexity
- Remains platform-agnostic
- Can recommend the best content regardless of where it lives
- Drives traffic to existing legitimate providers
