# CineVerse AI — AI Match Score

Every recommendation generated by CineVerse AI includes a **confidence score** that indicates how well the content aligns with the user's Entertainment DNA.

---

## How It Works

The AI evaluates genre affinity, behavioral signals, mood context, and collaborative patterns to calculate a percentage match for each recommendation.

---

## Example Scores

| Content | Match Score |
|---------|------------|
| Inception | 98% Match |
| Blade Runner 2049 | 95% Match |
| Ghost in the Shell | 91% Match |

---

## Explainability

The system does not just display a score — it explains **why** the recommendation was generated.

Example explanation:

> *"Recommended because you enjoyed Interstellar and rated Dune highly. 94% of users with your profile loved this film."*

This transparency builds trust and helps users understand their own preferences more clearly.
