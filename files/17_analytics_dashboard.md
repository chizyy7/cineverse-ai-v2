# CineVerse AI — Analytics Dashboard

Every user receives a personal analytics dashboard that provides insights into their entertainment habits and preferences.

---

## Dashboard Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Most Watched Genre | The genre the user engages with most |
| Monthly Listening Time | Total time spent on music and podcasts |
| Favorite Actors | Actors who appear most in the user's saved content |
| Favorite Artists | Musicians the user listens to most frequently |
| Recommendation Accuracy | How often the user rates recommended content positively |
| Entertainment Trends | How the user's tastes have shifted over time |

---

## Value to Users

The analytics dashboard helps users:

- Understand their own entertainment personality
- Track how their tastes evolve
- See patterns across different content types
- Measure how well the platform's recommendations align with their preferences

---

## Premium Analytics

Premium subscribers unlock deeper analytics including personalized entertainment reports and extended historical trend data.
